# DevRel / PMM / SE target list — 2026-04-28

10 hiring managers across AI coding companies. Each row: company, role to apply for, name, LinkedIn URL, current openings, hook line for the DM (something specific they posted/shipped/said), confidence (HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW based on fit + reachability).

**Search posture:** Russell's wedge is a PreToolUse firewall + 39 paired chaos runs showing 80/20 refusal asymmetry — that's a research/empirical narrative AI coding teams either already wanted or already wrote about. The post is shaped for the Claude Code team specifically; it generalizes for everyone else as "what your agent doesn't refuse on its own."

**Pattern noticed:** Most AI coding cos do NOT have a single visible "Director of DevRel" — DevRel is run by founders (Cline, Continue, Amp), or a Head of Education (Cursor, Anthropic), or it's distributed across several DevAdvocates with no clear lead (Vercel since Lee Robinson left). For 6 of 10 below, the right contact is the founder/CEO or a department head, NOT a traditional DevRel director.
